How to change your Zoom background on desktop:

  1. Download a background image to your computer.
  2. In Zoom settings, enable the Virtual Background option on the Meetings tab.
  3. While in a meeting click the ^ arrow next to the Start/Stop Video.
  4. Click the + button and upload the background from your computer.

How to change your Zoom background on mobile:

  1. Save a background image by holding down on the photo and selecting "Save to photos"
  2. When you log into your account and join a meeting, tap the three dots at the bottom right of the screen to open the More menu.
  3. Tap Virtual Background.
  4. Select your background photo.

Troubleshooting? Learn more on the Zoom support page
